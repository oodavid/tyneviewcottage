Things to do

The cottage is only 7 miles from Newcastle and yet in the heart of the countryside,
perfect for those who like walking, cycling and the occasional trip into the city.

Cycling and Walking

Keelmans Way is part of the vast cycle network in the North-East and passes your front door!

There are local circuits for every fitness, from 5-miles on the flat to gruelling hillclimbs in Northumberland

Golfing

There are no less than 4 golf clubs within walking distance: one of the most densely packed golfing areas in the country.

Tyneside Golf Club, Ryton Golf Club, Close House Golf Club and Prudhoe Golf Club.

The River

The cottage was once the Clubhouse of Tynemouth Rowing Club, it is possible to row the 7 miles to Newcastle Quayside or the 16 miles to Tynemouth.

Fishing is also possible with a licence

Real Ale

The The Boathouse in Wylam has 15 CAMRA awards and is a must for ale enthusiasts! A 45 minute walk up the river.

If that's too far there are pubs and shops in the local village Ryton.
